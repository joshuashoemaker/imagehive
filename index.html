How It Works!

Send

Complete your order online or print our order form to get started. Pack your box with your media and ship it to us. Not comfortable shipping? No problem! You can drop your media off in person at our lab in Birmingham, AL.

Convert

Once we receive your order, we will call to let you know it arrived safely and to discuss your order with you. Your memories are all then digitized at our lab here in Birmingham, AL by our skilled technicians.
